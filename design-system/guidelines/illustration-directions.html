Decision aid · treatment study

Three illustration directions

One subject — a pinned specimen moth — rendered three ways inside the real plate frame, so you compare the treatment, not the drawing.

These are CSS/SVG treatment studies, not final art: they show the visual register (line, tone, colour) each direction would carry. Once you pick one, the actual plates would be generated or commissioned to match — every entry's IllustrationPlate slot is already wired to take them.
