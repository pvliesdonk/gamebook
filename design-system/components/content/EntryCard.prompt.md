A field-guide plate as a card — for browse grids, related-entry rails, and "Referenced by" lists. Composes `MediaBadge` and `Tag`.

```jsx
<EntryCard
  number={14}
  taxon="Puzzles · Cluing"
  title="The Fairness Contract"
  silhouette="Every locked door has a findable key."
  tags={['Cluing', 'Fairness']}
  media="if"
/>
```

Amber top-rule and hover lift mark it as a plate. Omit any field to drop that row.
